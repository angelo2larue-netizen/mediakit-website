01 — MANIFESTO / EST. 2024
● SHOT IN TOKYO

“Yukia isn't a brand.
It's a refusal.”

We make clothes for people who don't dress for approval.

Every Yukia piece is born from late nights, blank pages, and the kind of obsession that doesn't sleep. We don't follow trends — we fossilize them. Every drop is limited. Every silhouette is intentional. When it's gone, it's gone forever.

We pull from the raw energy of Y2K subculture, the precision of Japanese tailoring, and the unfiltered attitude of street royalty. The result is a uniform for the ones who refuse to blend in.
