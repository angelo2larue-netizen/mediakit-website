§ 01

Who we are

YUKIA® is a streetwear studio based between Tokyo and Paris, operated by YUKIA Studio SAS. When we say "we", "us", or "YUKIA" in this document, that's who we mean. When we say "you", we mean anyone who visits yukia.vercel.app, places an order, or joins the Inner Circle.

This policy explains what we do with your personal data, why we do it, and the rights you have over it. It's written to be read — no dark patterns, no legal smoke.

§ 02

Data we collect

We only ask for what we actually need. Depending on how you interact with us, that may include:

  • Identity & contact — name, email, shipping address, phone (only if required for delivery).
  • Order data — items purchased, size, price, order history.
  • Payment data — handled directly by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Technical data — browser type, device, referring URL, anonymized analytics.
  • Marketing preferences — your Inner Circle status, drop alert opt-ins.
§ 03

How we use it

Your data is used strictly to: fulfill your orders, provide customer support, send drop alerts you've opted into, and improve the YUKIA experience. We don't sell it. We don't rent it. We don't hand it to data brokers.

Legal basis: performance of contract (orders), legitimate interest (site security, fraud prevention), and consent (marketing).

§ 04

Sharing & third parties

We only share data with trusted partners required to run the drops:

  • Stripe — payment processing (PCI-DSS certified).
  • Shipping carriers — to deliver your order.
  • Vercel — hosting infrastructure.
  • Email platform — for drop alerts (opt-in only).

All partners are contractually bound to process your data only for the specific task we assign them.

§ 05

Your rights

Under GDPR and equivalent regulations, you can at any time: access the data we hold on you, correct it, delete it, export it in a portable format, restrict how we use it, or withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@yukia.studio and we'll respond within 30 days.

§ 06

Retention

Order data is kept for 10 years as required by French commercial law. Marketing data is kept until you unsubscribe. Technical logs are kept for 13 months maximum. After that, everything is deleted or fully anonymized.

§ 07

Security

All traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.3. Payments run through Stripe's PCI-DSS infrastructure. Access to customer data is restricted to a handful of authorized staff. In the unlikely event of a breach, we'll notify affected users within 72 hours as required by law.
